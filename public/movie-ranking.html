The Best Movies According to Alejandro

My Top 3 Movies of ALL Time

Princess Mononoke

This is my first ever favorite anime movie, the story is meaningful and the art style is amazing

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba - The Movie: Mugen Train

The first movie to one of my favorite anime series. The characters are likeable and the art style is one of my favorites. Sad ending.

Akira

I know this is an oldie, but didn't discover it till I was much older. Great classic!
